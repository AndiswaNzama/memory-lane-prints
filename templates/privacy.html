Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information

Compliant with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), 2013

Last updated: May 2025, Version 1.0

1. Who We Are

Memory Lane Prints is a South African business specialising in personalised photo books and keepsakes. We are the responsible party as defined under POPIA and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Information Officer contact:
Email: hello@memorylaneprints.co.za

2. What Personal Information We Collect

When you place an order, we collect the following personal information:

  • Full name:for delivery labelling and correspondence
  • Email address:for order confirmation and shipping updates
  • Phone number:for delivery contact and support
  • Delivery address:to send your physical order
  • Uploaded photos:to create your personalised photo book
  • Special notes:layout preferences, captions, and occasion details you provide

If you leave a review, we also collect your rating, review text, and an optional photo of your book.

3. Why We Collect Your Information

We only collect personal information for the following purposes:

  • To process and fulfil your order
  • To communicate with you about your order status and delivery
  • To provide customer support
  • To display your review on our website (only if approved and submitted voluntarily)
  • To comply with our legal obligations

We do not use your information for marketing unless you have separately opted in.

4. How We Store and Protect Your Information

Your personal details are stored in a secure database hosted on Railway (South African region). Your uploaded photos are stored on Cloudinary, a secure cloud platform, and are used solely for producing your order.

Payments are processed by PayFast, a PCI-DSS compliant South African payment gateway. We never store your card details.

Access to your personal information is restricted to the business owner and authorised staff only. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to prevent unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

5. Sharing Your Information

We will never sell or rent your personal information. We only share it with:

  • Courier services (Pudo / Paxi) — your name and delivery address, to fulfil delivery
  • PayFast:your name and email, to process payment securely
  • Cloudinary:your uploaded photos, for storage and order production
  • Law enforcement or regulators:only if required by South African law

6. How Long We Keep Your Information

We retain your order information (including personal details and photos) for up to 3 years after your order is delivered, for accounting and dispute resolution purposes. After this period, your data is securely deleted. You may request earlier deletion (see below).

7. Your Rights Under POPIA

As a data subject under the Protection of Personal Information Act (Act 4 of 2013), you have the right to:

  • Access:request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction:request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion:request that we delete your personal information (subject to legal obligations)
  • Objection:object to the processing of your personal information
  • Complaint:lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@memorylaneprints.co.za with your order number and request. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Information Regulator

If you believe your rights have been violated, you may contact the Information Regulator of South Africa:

  • Website: inforegulator.org.za
  • Email: inforeg@justice.gov.za

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The version number and date at the top of this page indicate the current revision. Continued use of our services after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
