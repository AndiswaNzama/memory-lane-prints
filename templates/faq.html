Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your Memory Lane Prints book

Ordering

Choose your package, upload your photos, fill in your details, and complete payment via PayFast. You'll receive a confirmation email with your order reference number straight away.
Once payment is confirmed, production begins immediately, so we cannot accept changes. Please review your photos, personalisation text, and delivery address carefully before paying.

Photos

For the best print quality, we recommend photos that are at least 1MB in size. Most modern smartphone photos work perfectly. We accept JPG, PNG, HEIC, and WEBP formats. If a photo is too low resolution to print well, we'll contact you before proceeding.
It depends on your package:
  • Mini Memories (24 pages): up to 30 photos
  • Signature Storybook (32–40 pages): up to 50 photos
  • Luxury Keepsake (50+ pages): up to 80 photos
If you add extra pages, you can include more. Upload as many as you'd like and we'll curate the best selection.
We don't currently offer digital proofs, but we review every order carefully before printing. If anything looks off with your photos or layout instructions, we'll contact you before proceeding.

Production & Delivery

Most orders are designed, printed, and dispatched within 5–10 business days of payment. Delivery via courier then takes 2–5 business days depending on your location. You'll receive an email with your tracking number as soon as your book is dispatched.
Yes, we deliver nationwide via Pudo and Paxi courier networks, which cover all major towns and cities across South Africa.
Use the Track Order page and enter your order reference number. You'll see your current status, and once dispatched, your courier tracking number will appear there too.

Returns & Refunds

Because every book is made uniquely for you, we do not accept returns for change of mind. However, if there is a production defect or printing error on our side, please email us at hello@memorylaneprints.co.za within 7 days of receiving your book, with photos of the issue. We'll reprint or refund with no hassle.
